# Simple 3-bit v1 test program
# tests all opcodes in 3-bit v1 ISA


111 001 001 001 # 0 - no op
001 101 011 000 # 1 - Move literal 3 into RAM address 5
001 110 010 000 # 2 - Move literal 2 into RAM address 6
011 111 101 110 # 3 - Sub RAM[5] + RAM[6] and store result in RAM[7]
010 111 101 110 # 4 - Add RAM[5] + RAM[6] and store result in RAM[7]
100 010 101 110 # 5 - compare addr 5 (3) and addr 6 (2)
110 000 001 000 # 6 - jgt addr 5 (3) > addr 6 (2) ? jmp 8
001 001 001 000 # 7 - Move literal 1 into RAM address 1 (this line should get skipped)
001 001 010 000 # 8 - Move literal 2 into RAM address 1
111 001 001 001 # 9 - no op
100 010 101 101 # 10 - compare addr 5 == addr 5
101 000 001 101 # 11 - jeq addr 5 == addr 5 ? jmp 13 (unconditional jump)
001 001 011 000 # 12 - Move literal 3 into RAM address 1 (this line should get skipped)
110 000 001 111 # 13 - jgt 15
001 001 100 000 # 14 - Move literal 4 into RAM address 1
001 001 111 001 # 15 - Move literal 7 into RAM address 9 (page 2 addr 1)
000 000 000 000 # 16 - Halt execution
